About The Team!

Driven by our love of a good cocktail, our team formed to find a better way of choosing the right cocktail for the right time. Using the latest public technologies, we've developed a custom algorithm that combines self-provided data with face tracking interfaces to better bring drink recommendations to the masses. Our goal is for people to drink the right cocktail to match their mood in an effort to make the world a better place.
